Discover Wicklow

Wicklow Harbour

One of Ireland's coastal treasures. Take a walk along the harbour piers,
enjoy the fine local seafood and experience the serenity of the sea.

Wicklow Golf Club

Tee off against the backdrop of stunning coastal views at Wicklow Golf Club.
An equally challenging and beautiful links course, Wicklow Golf Club will take your breath away.

Wicklow's Historic Gaol

Step back in time at Wicklow's Historic Gaol. One of Ireland's top tourist attractions,
Wicklow Gaol tells the story of its prisoners through an interactive tour led by
experienced and passionate actor guides.

Wicklow's Black Castle

Explore the ruins of Wicklow's Black Castle, perched dramatically on the coast.
Discover the historic past of this once great fortress as you stare over the Irish Sea.

The Garden of Ireland

Welcome to Wicklow, the "Garden of Ireland". Located 30 minutes south of Dublin City, Wicklow combines vibrant landscapes with a storied history. Step into Ireland's rich past in Wicklow, located at the heart of "Ireland's Ancient East". Explore ancient settlements like Glendalough, or delve into the past at Wicklow's Historic Gaol. Marvel at the grandeur of estates like Russborough and Killruddery, or get lost in the stunning beauty of Powerscourt and Mount Usher gardens.
Wicklow is Ireland's premier outdoor destination. Whether you enjoy hiking, cycling, or horse riding through breathtaking scenery, Wicklow caters to all adventurers. Tee off on world-class golf courses, or cast your line along the coastline, rivers, and lakes teaming with fish. Boasting Ireland's largest national park, expansive forests, towering mountains, and the tallest waterfall in Ireland, Wicklow is a paradise for nature lovers.
